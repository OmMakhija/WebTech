Mathematical Formulas

Formula Mathematical Expression
a. Area of a circle: πr²
b. Trigonometric Identities with angle α: Sine (sinα): sin(α)
Cosine (cosα): cos(α)
Tangent (tanα): tan(α)
c. Square of (a+b): (a+b)² = a² + 2ab + b²
d. Cube of (a+b): (a+b)³ = a³ + 3a²b + 3ab² + b³
e. Difference between squares of two variables: a² - b²
f. Time Period of a pendulum: T = 2π√(l/g)
g. Gravitational Force: F = (G * m1 * m2) / r²
h. Kinetic Energy: KE = ½mv²
i. Relation between mass and Energy: E = mc²
j. Relation between density (ρ), mass (m), and Volume (V): ρ = m/V
